Air University

A Societal Mission

Air University, established in 2002 and located in Islamabad, Pakistan, is a leading institution dedicated to providing quality education and research opportunities. Affiliated with the Pakistan Air Force, it offers a diverse range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as engineering, computer science, business, and social sciences.The university emphasizes a blend of academic excellence, innovation, and integrity, preparing students to become skilled professionals and leaders. With a strong focus on research and development, Air University contributes significantly to technological advancements and societal progress, aiming to shape a brighter future for Pakistan and the global community.
